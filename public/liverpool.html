Liverpool Building Services FAQ

Frequently asked questions about our Liverpool building services

What Liverpool building services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive Liverpool building services including house extensions, complete renovations, new builds, loft conversions, kitchen fitting, bathroom fitting, electrical work, and roofing. Our Liverpool builders cover all areas including West Derby, Crosby, Formby, Southport, Woolton, and Liverpool city centre.

Do you need planning permission for Liverpool house extensions?

Many Liverpool house extensions fall under Permitted Development Rights and don't require planning permission. However, larger extensions, those in conservation areas, or on listed buildings typically require planning permission from Liverpool City Council. Our Liverpool team provides full planning support and guidance.

How much do Liverpool building services cost?

Liverpool building costs vary depending on the project scope. Single-storey extensions typically range from £45,000-£75,000, while complete Liverpool house renovations can range from £70,000-£180,000+. We provide free Liverpool quotes and transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

Are you CITB certified Liverpool builders?

Yes, all our Liverpool builders are CITB certified with current CSCS cards. We're fully insured with comprehensive public liability and employer's liability insurance. Our Liverpool building team meets all current building regulations and safety standards.

What areas of Liverpool do you cover?

We cover all Liverpool areas including West Derby (L12), Crosby (L23), Formby (L37), Southport (PR8-9), Woolton (L25), Liverpool city centre (L1-L8), Aigburth (L17), Allerton (L18), and all Merseyside postcodes. Contact our Liverpool team for your area.

How long do Liverpool building projects take?

Liverpool building project timelines vary: single-storey extensions typically take 6-10 weeks, double-storey extensions 10-16 weeks, loft conversions 4-8 weeks, and complete renovations 12-24 weeks. Our Liverpool project managers provide detailed timelines for each project.
