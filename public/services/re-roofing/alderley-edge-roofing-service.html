Common Roofing Issues in Alderley Edge

We resolve common problems quickly to prevent costly damage

Persistent Leaks

Water ingress around chimneys, valleys or rooflights. We locate the source and apply lasting repairs.

Storm Damage

High winds can loosen tiles and ridge caps. We provide emergency make-safe and follow-up repairs.

Moss Build-Up

Moss traps moisture and stresses tiles. We clean, treat and fit preventative measures where needed.

Chimney & Leadwork

Failed flashings and mortar joints are common leak points. We repoint and replace leadwork to spec.
