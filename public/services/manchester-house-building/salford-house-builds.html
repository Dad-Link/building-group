Salford New Build FAQ

Common questions about building new homes in Salford

How long does it take to build a new house in Salford?

Build times vary by size and complexity. Typical 3–4 bedroom homes take 20–28 weeks from start to completion. Larger or more complex homes may take 30–40 weeks. We provide a programme tailored to your Salford site.

Do you manage planning with Salford City Council?

Yes. We handle architectural drawings, planning applications, and liaison with Salford City Council to streamline approvals across the city.

What’s included in your Salford new build service?

Complete turnkey service: design, planning, building regs, groundworks, structure, MEP, internal fit-out, and full project management—tailored to Salford standards and sites.

Can I customize the design of my Salford home?

Absolutely. We specialize in bespoke homes tailored to your lifestyle, from layouts and architectural style to material choices and finishes.

What warranty do you provide on new builds?

10-year structural warranty, 2 years on internal finishes, and 1 year on fixtures and fittings, plus ongoing maintenance support.

How much does it cost to build a house in Salford?

Standard builds from £1,200/m², premium from £1,650/m², and luxury from £2,400/m². A typical 2,000 sq ft home ranges from £240,000 to £480,000 depending on specification and site conditions.
