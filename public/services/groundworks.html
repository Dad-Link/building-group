Foundation Types & Solutions

Professional foundation solutions for all construction projects

Strip Foundations

Traditional concrete strip foundations ideal for most domestic and light commercial construction. Cost-effective solution for standard soil conditions.

  • Domestic construction
  • Load-bearing walls
  • Standard soil conditions
  • Cost-effective solution

Pad Foundations

Reinforced concrete pads for isolated loads such as columns and pillars. Perfect for steel frame buildings and structural support points.

  • Column support
  • Steel frame construction
  • Isolated loads
  • Reinforced concrete

Piled Foundations

Deep foundation systems for challenging ground conditions or heavy loads. Mini-piled and driven pile solutions for maximum structural integrity.

  • Poor ground conditions
  • Heavy structural loads
  • Deep foundation system
  • Maximum stability

Raft Foundations

Large concrete slabs distributing loads over wide areas. Ideal for weak soils or where settlement needs to be minimized across the structure.

  • Weak soil conditions
  • Large structural loads
  • Settlement prevention
  • Wide load distribution
