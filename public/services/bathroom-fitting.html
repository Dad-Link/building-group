Bathroom Fitting FAQ

Common questions about our bathroom installation services

How long does a bathroom installation take?

A standard bathroom installation typically takes 7-10 working days. This includes removal of the old bathroom, plumbing and electrical work, tiling, and installation of all new fixtures. Complex installations or structural changes may take 10-14 days.

Do you handle all the plumbing and electrical work?

Yes, our team includes qualified plumbers and electricians who handle all aspects of the installation. All electrical work is carried out by Part P certified electricians and comes with appropriate certificates and warranties.

What's included in your bathroom fitting service?

Our complete service includes: design consultation, removal of existing bathroom, plumbing and electrical work, waterproofing, tiling, installation of all fixtures and fittings, painting/decorating, and final cleanup. We handle everything from start to finish.

Can you work with my own bathroom suite?

Absolutely! We're happy to install bathroom suites that you've purchased separately. We can advise on compatibility and suitability, and our installation warranty still applies to our workmanship.

Do you provide a warranty on your work?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on all our bathroom installations: 2-5 years on our workmanship (depending on package), manufacturer warranties on all products, and ongoing support for any issues that may arise.

Can you install disabled access bathrooms?

Yes, we specialize in accessible bathroom installations including wet rooms, walk-in showers, grab rails, raised toilets, and other accessibility features. We work to current disability access guidelines and can advise on grant availability.
