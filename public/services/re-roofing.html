Signs Your Roof Needs Replacement

Don't ignore these warning signs - early intervention can prevent costly damage

Leaks & Water Damage

Water stains on ceilings, damp patches, or actual leaks indicate roof failure requiring immediate attention to prevent structural damage.

Missing or Damaged Tiles

Cracked, broken, or missing tiles allow water penetration and can lead to more extensive roof damage if not addressed promptly.

Age of Roof

Roofs over 20-30 years old (depending on material) should be professionally inspected and may require complete replacement.

Storm Damage

High winds, heavy rain, or hail can cause significant roof damage that may not be immediately visible but requires professional assessment.

Moss & Vegetation Growth

Excessive moss growth indicates moisture retention and potential roof deck damage requiring professional cleaning and repairs.

Sagging or Structural Issues

Visible sagging, uneven roof lines, or structural movement indicates serious problems requiring immediate professional assessment.
