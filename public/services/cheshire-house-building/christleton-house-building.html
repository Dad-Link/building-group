Christleton New Builds – FAQ

Planning, cost, timeline and sustainability

Do you help source plots and undertake feasibility in Christleton?

Yes. We can review potential sites, assess access/services, planning context and produce a feasibility report with outline budgets.

Can you deliver shell-only with client fit-out?

We offer flexible contracts: shell & core, watertight stage, or full turnkey including interiors, kitchens and landscaping.

What sustainability standards can you meet?

We target high fabric performance, low air permeability, and integrate heat pumps, MVHR and PV to achieve strong SAP/EPC results or Passivhaus principles.
