Signs Your Property Needs Rewiring

Don't ignore these warning signs - they could indicate dangerous electrical faults

Flickering or Dimming Lights

Lights that flicker, dim, or brighten unexpectedly indicate overloaded circuits or loose connections that need immediate attention.

Burning Smells or Scorch Marks

Any burning smells from sockets, switches, or the consumer unit, or scorch marks around electrical fittings require emergency rewiring.

Frequent Electrical Shocks

Getting shocks from switches, sockets, or appliances indicates earthing problems that pose serious safety risks.

Circuit Breakers Tripping

If your RCD or circuit breakers trip frequently, it usually indicates circuit overload or earth leakage requiring professional investigation.

Wiring Over 30 Years Old

Electrical wiring installed before 1990 may not meet current safety standards and should be professionally assessed and upgraded.

Outdated Electrical Systems

Fuse boxes instead of modern consumer units, rubber or fabric-covered cables, or round-pin sockets all indicate urgent rewiring needs.
