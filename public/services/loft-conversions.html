Types of Loft Conversions

Choose the perfect conversion type to maximize your loft space and property value

Velux Loft Conversion

The most cost-effective option using roof windows for natural light. Perfect for properties with adequate headroom and no structural changes needed.

  • Most affordable option
  • Minimal structural work
  • No planning permission usually required
  • Natural light from roof windows

Dormer Loft Conversion

Add valuable floor space and headroom with single or multiple dormers. Popular choice offering excellent space optimization and natural light.

  • Maximum space gain
  • Improved headroom
  • Additional windows
  • Cost-effective space solution

Hip-to-Gable Conversion

Transform sloping hip roofs into vertical gable walls, significantly increasing usable floor space. Ideal for terraced and semi-detached properties.

  • Substantial space increase
  • Improved room proportions
  • Better natural light
  • Enhanced property value

Mansard Loft Conversion

The premium option creating maximum space with near-vertical walls. Perfect for period properties and achieving substantial additional floor area.

  • Maximum floor space
  • Multiple rooms possible
  • Period property suitable
  • Highest value addition

Loft Conversion Requirements

Your loft needs minimum 2.3m headroom at the highest point and sufficient floor area for conversion. Our specialists provide free assessments to determine if your loft is suitable and advise on the best conversion type for your property and budget. Most conversions fall under permitted development rights, but we handle all planning and building regulations applications where required.
