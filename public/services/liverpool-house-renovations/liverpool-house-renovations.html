Comprehensive Liverpool Refurbishments

We deliver seamless Liverpool house renovations, from strip-out and structural changes to first/second fix M&E, plastering, flooring and final finishes. Our project managers coordinate trades, maintain safety and quality, and provide clear timelines and communication.

We understand Liverpool’s conservation considerations, period property details and Building Control requirements, ensuring compliant renovations with excellent craftsmanship.

  • Full Refurbishments – Reconfigure layouts, upgrade services
  • Structural Alterations – Load-bearing wall removal, steel/RSJ
  • Kitchens & Bathrooms – Design, supply, install and finish
  • Electrical & Plumbing – Certified rewire and re-plumb
  • Plastering & Decorating – Skim, tape, paint, finishes
  • Period Restoration – Heritage-sensitive detailing

Liverpool Renovations – FAQ

Planning, costs, timelines and regulations

Do you provide design and layout advice?

Yes, we advise on layouts, structural options and specification to meet your budget and goals. We can provide drawings where required.

Can you coordinate kitchens and bathrooms?

Absolutely. We handle joinery, plumbing, electrics, tiling and finishes, coordinating suppliers and ensuring a seamless result.

Are all trades certified?

Yes. Electrical and gas works are certified and included in your handover pack along with building control documentation.
