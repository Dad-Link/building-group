Types of House Extensions

Choose the perfect extension type to suit your home and lifestyle

Single-Storey Extensions

Perfect for expanding ground floor living spaces. Ideal for kitchen extensions, dining areas, or family rooms with seamless indoor-outdoor flow.

  • Kitchen extensions
  • Family room additions
  • Bi-fold doors to garden
  • Open-plan living

Double-Storey Extensions

Maximize space with two-storey additions. Add bedrooms, bathrooms, and living areas while maintaining garden space and adding significant value.

  • Extra bedrooms
  • Additional bathrooms
  • Expanded living areas
  • Maximum value addition

Wrap-Around Extensions

Combine side return and rear extensions for maximum space transformation. Create spectacular open-plan living areas with abundant natural light.

  • Maximum space gain
  • Open-plan design
  • Natural light optimization
  • Complete transformation

Kitchen Extensions

Transform your kitchen into the heart of your home. Modern kitchen extensions with island units, dining areas, and seamless garden access.

  • Modern kitchen design
  • Island and peninsula units
  • Dining integration
  • Garden connectivity

Conservatories & Orangeries

Light-filled extensions that blur the boundary between indoor and outdoor living. Modern glazed extensions with excellent thermal performance.

  • Maximum natural light
  • Garden views
  • Year-round usability
  • Thermal efficiency

Loft Extensions

Convert unused loft space into bedrooms, offices, or playrooms. Dormer extensions and full loft conversions with modern amenities.

  • Additional bedrooms
  • Home office space
  • En-suite bathrooms
  • Dormer windows

Benefits of House Extensions

Increase Property Value

Well-designed extensions can add 15-25% to your property value, often exceeding the construction cost and providing excellent return on investment.

Avoid Moving Costs

Extend your current home instead of moving. Save on estate agent fees, stamp duty, legal costs, and the stress of relocating.

Better Family Living

Create the space your family needs to grow and thrive. Open-plan living, additional bedrooms, and functional family areas.

Personalized Design

Design your extension exactly how you want it. Custom layouts, premium materials, and finishes that reflect your style and needs.
