Manchester Building Services FAQ

Frequently asked questions about our Manchester building services

What Manchester building services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive Manchester building services including house extensions, complete renovations, new builds, loft conversions, kitchen fitting, bathroom fitting, electrical work, and roofing. Our Manchester builders cover all areas including Didsbury, Altrincham, Sale, Stockport, Chorlton, and Manchester city centre.

Do you need planning permission for Manchester house extensions?

Many Manchester house extensions fall under Permitted Development Rights and don't require planning permission. However, larger extensions, those in conservation areas, or on listed buildings typically require planning permission from Manchester City Council. Our Manchester team provides full planning support and guidance.

How much do Manchester building services cost?

Manchester building costs vary depending on the project scope. Single-storey extensions typically range from £50,000-£80,000, while complete Manchester house renovations can range from £80,000-£200,000+. We provide free Manchester quotes and transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

Are you CITB certified Manchester builders?

Yes, all our Manchester builders are CITB certified with current CSCS cards. We're fully insured with comprehensive public liability and employer's liability insurance. Our Manchester building team meets all current building regulations and safety standards.

What areas of Manchester do you cover?

We cover all Manchester areas including Didsbury (M20), Altrincham (WA14), Sale (M33), Stockport (SK1-8), Chorlton (M21), Manchester city centre (M1-15), Wilmslow (SK9), Bramhall (SK7), and all Greater Manchester postcodes. Contact our Manchester team for your area.

How long do Manchester building projects take?

Manchester building project timelines vary: single-storey extensions typically take 6-10 weeks, double-storey extensions 10-16 weeks, loft conversions 4-8 weeks, and complete renovations 12-24 weeks. Our Manchester project managers provide detailed timelines for each project.
