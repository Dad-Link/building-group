Our Story

Building Group was founded in 2008 with a simple vision: to become the North West's most trusted construction company by delivering exceptional quality, reliability, and customer service on every project.

Starting as a small team of skilled tradespeople in Manchester, we have grown to become a leading construction contractor serving clients across Greater Manchester, Merseyside, West Yorkshire, and Cheshire. Our success is built on strong foundations - skilled craftsmanship, professional integrity, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, we're proud to be CITB certified contractors with a portfolio spanning new builds, renovations, extensions, and specialist construction services. Every project, from small domestic repairs to large commercial developments, receives the same attention to detail and professional standard that has earned us our reputation.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to North West England's leading construction group

2008

Company Founded

Building Group established in Manchester with a small team of skilled tradespeople focused on domestic construction and renovations.

2011

CITB Certification

Achieved CITB certification and expanded services to include commercial construction projects across Greater Manchester.

2014

Regional Expansion

Extended operations to Liverpool and Merseyside, establishing our reputation for quality construction services across the North West.

2017

Yorkshire Operations

Opened Leeds office and began serving West Yorkshire, bringing our expertise to the growing Yorkshire construction market.

Cheshire Services
