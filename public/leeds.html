Leeds Building Services FAQ

Frequently asked questions about our Leeds building services

What Leeds building services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive Leeds building services including house extensions, complete renovations, new builds, loft conversions, kitchen fitting, bathroom fitting, electrical work, and roofing. Our Leeds builders cover all areas including Harrogate, Roundhay, Horsforth, Wetherby, Chapel Allerton, and Leeds city centre.

Do you need planning permission for Leeds house extensions?

Many Leeds house extensions fall under Permitted Development Rights and don't require planning permission. However, larger extensions, those in conservation areas, or on listed buildings typically require planning permission from Leeds City Council. Our Leeds team provides full planning support and guidance.

How much do Leeds building services cost?

Leeds building costs vary depending on the project scope. Single-storey extensions typically range from £45,000-£75,000, while complete Leeds house renovations can range from £70,000-£180,000+. We provide free Leeds quotes and transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

Are you CITB certified Leeds builders?

Yes, all our Leeds builders are CITB certified with current CSCS cards. We're fully insured with comprehensive public liability and employer's liability insurance. Our Leeds building team meets all current building regulations and safety standards.

What areas of Leeds do you cover?

We cover all Leeds areas including Harrogate (HG1-3), Roundhay (LS8), Horsforth (LS18), Wetherby (LS22), Chapel Allerton (LS7), Leeds city centre (LS1-6), Headingley (LS6), Ilkley (LS29), and all West Yorkshire postcodes. Contact our Leeds team for your area.

How long do Leeds building projects take?

Leeds building project timelines vary: single-storey extensions typically take 6-10 weeks, double-storey extensions 10-16 weeks, loft conversions 4-8 weeks, and complete renovations 12-24 weeks. Our Leeds project managers provide detailed timelines for each project.
