Cheshire Building Services FAQ

Frequently asked questions about our Cheshire luxury building services

What Cheshire building services do you offer?

Do you need planning permission for Cheshire house extensions?

Many Cheshire house extensions fall under Permitted Development Rights and don't require planning permission. However, larger extensions, those in conservation areas, or on listed buildings typically require planning permission from Cheshire East or Cheshire West councils. Our Cheshire team provides full planning support and guidance.

How much do Cheshire luxury building services cost?

Cheshire luxury building costs vary depending on the project scope and specifications. Single-storey extensions typically range from £60,000-£120,000, while complete Cheshire luxury renovations can range from £100,000-£300,000+. We provide free Cheshire quotes and transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

Yes, all our Cheshire builders are CITB certified with current CSCS cards. We're fully insured with comprehensive public liability and employer's liability insurance. Our Cheshire building team meets all current building regulations and safety standards for luxury construction.
